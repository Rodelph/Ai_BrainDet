Informations about the project


Cancer, or tumors in general have always been one of the hardest diseases that a Human can fight, patient and doctor wise. It has one of the most painful and longest medecial treatment procedures that can be mentally, physically, and financially painful. Doctors and researchers always tried new technologies, and innovated their ways to find new and better methods to improve the efficiency of the treatement, making it easier for the patient, as well as making it safer.


But one aspect was really hard to improve which is locating the tumor with pixel precision due to technological boundaries. Processing images with high resolution was a challenge to hardware that dated from decades ago, but not today. Hardware, thanks to many companies has improved greatly, and what was impossible 10 years ago, can be now achieved is minutes. Deep-Learning is a task that is run on Graphical Cards, and better GPUs will always lead to a better predictions, and faster compilations.


This is a project that demonstrates the power of AI in the Medical field, which i personally consider one of the driving factors to a new era in the technological aspects of fighting tumors. Combining the power of Deep-Learning, Image processing will and always lead to great results.


This project was made using Python, Keras, Flask, OpenCV, as a well as an Image database collected from Kaggle. The main objective of this project is not only to detect if the program is able to determine if the brain MRI image contains a tumor, but also to locate that tumor in that same image.
